The Department of Electronics Engineering was established in the year 1984. The Department offers a course of six semesters leading to a diploma in Electronics Engineering which is affiliated to AICTE....The Department of Electronics Engineering was established in the year 1984. The Department offers a course of six semesters leading to a diploma in Electronics Engineering which is affiliated to AICTE.The students for the diploma program are admitted based on the rank list prepared based on index score obtained in the Xth class. The annual intake is 60 students. The students get placement through the Career Guidance and Placement Cell (CGPC) of the College. Most of the alumni of the college are currently working in reputed national and international organizations. Some of them pursued for B Tech through lateral entry. The Faculty of the department is the best of the lot. The Department has full- fledged laboratories. The department periodically organizes seminars and workshops to train students in the area of Electronics Engineering. Faculties from premier institutions are invited to the department for lectures under visiting faculty scheme. Department conducts periodic reviews by departmental committee and class PTA. Evaluation of faculty by students are performed once in a semester.
