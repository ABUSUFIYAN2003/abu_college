It offeres diploma program with an yearly intake of 60 students. Today the Department has a vibrant student body numbering about 150. The computing facility of the Department consists of more than 100 systems: two software labs running with Linux & Windows servers and one fully equipped hardware lab. The internet lab has a capacity of 25 systems with high speed internet connectivity and wifi facility. The Department also coordinates several co-curricular activities/trainings like Spoken Tutorial project- conducted by IIT Bombay, an initiative of National Mission on Education through ICT, MHRD, Govt. of India.
